# Novelty Boundary Map

Inside the boundary:

- Hidden fixture/support modes that are not visible in the initial scene representation.
- Outcome residuals from small probes as evidence for those modes.
- A policy interface where the inferred mode selects release, reorientation, or direct manipulation.

Outside the boundary:

- General scene graph learning.
- Generic latent world models without fixture semantics.
- Fixture-aware assembly planning when the fixture is already specified.
- Pure tactile material classification without a downstream manipulation-mode choice.
- Unknown fixture discovery when the taxonomy and action menu are incomplete.
